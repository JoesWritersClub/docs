  • Code of Conduct

Membership to Joe’s Writers’ Club is available to writers of all ages and to  minors with their guardian's consent/supervision. In the Member’s Forum, Public Forum, and  Private Forums, members  are expected to adhere to an ethical and respectful  code of conduct that focuses on subject matter appropriate  for  a general public forum.  

Adult-appropriate subject matter in Private Forums may qualify as an exception when all members of that group will have verified they are of legal age. To proceed with membership, members must read and agree to the Code of Conduct Form.  Members must also read and agree to the JWC - XYZ  Non-disclosure Agreement, which restricts members from copying or sharing the work of other writers of the club without the expressed permission.

   

  • Benefits of XYZ Forums

        Membership to the JWC - XYZ Forums is completely free. Here we find camradare in and keep up to date with our writing community.  When acting as an outstanding member, we are invited to join private groups. These are teams of other writers who nurture our creativity and craft, and reinforce our skill in analysis. All while receiving the very same care from other members. In these teams we have club meetings, and share Google Docs and Drive folders to provide feedback to each other as we endeavour to create exemplary writings and publications.

  • Outstanding Members

        We guarantee that every member of Joe’s Writers’ Club will be or become an outstanding member and  writer. We must lead by example and follow in admiration. We have pulled from the best of our members’ experience and talents, and learned from the methods of several writer’s collectives who have joined forces to form this community. When you take part in a club meeting of Joe’s Writers’ Club you will inspire your muse and exercise your critical thinking; and you will surely become a better writer for it.
